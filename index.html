ABOUT THE UNDIE RUN

Team R.E.D. is excited to host our 2nd Annual Underwear One-Mile at the Bears Den in Byron on Dec 4th at 6:30pm.

Yes! You have fun while running 1-mile in your undies outside in December in Minnesota! All the while, helping a great cause! Proceeds go to Join the Journey and their mission of Supporting Individuals on their Breast Cancer Journey.

THE AFTER PARTY

Join us at the Bears Den for a post race celebration and a chance to win some great door prizes! A unique race day give-away is available for the first 50 registrants only.

ABOUT
Team R.E.D

Team R.E.D. is a club dedicated to having fun while staying fit! We say: Run. Eat. Drink. Two Out Of Three Ain't Bad! We meet twice a month at the Bears Den in Byron for a walk or run, and twice a month at a rotating location. We offer various routes each time, with distances suitable for walkers and runners. We have members of all paces.. all are welcome!


For more information, find us on facebook or send us an email at byron.teamred@gmail.com
