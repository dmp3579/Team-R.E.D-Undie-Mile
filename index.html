Run. Eat. Drink. Support Pink!

Dec 4th at 6:30pm, $25 to enter!

WHAT?

Team R.E.D. is excited to host our 2nd Annual Underwear One-Mile at the Bears Den in Byron on Dec 4th at 6:30pm.

WHY?

Yes! You have fun while running 1-mile in your undies outside in December in Minnesota!   All the while, helping a great cause!  Proceeds go to Join the Journey and their mission of Supporting Individuals on their Breast Cancer Journey.
Join us at the Bears Den for a post race celebration and a chance to win some great door prizes!
A unique race day give-away is available for the first 50 registrants only.

WHERE?

The Bears Den is located at 845 High Point Place NE, Byron MN.
